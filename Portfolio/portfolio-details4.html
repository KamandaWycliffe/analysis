Context

In the jewelry business, various factors determine the quality, price, and hence demand of associated jewelry or to put it more colloquially, the value of jewelry and more specifically diamonds in the current workâ€™s case is influenced by among other factors rarity, cut, clarity, color, and carat as observed by (Ely, 2021). Often, to understand the underlying associations between various aspects of jewelry and its value, it is important to conduct different analyses either by distribution, general correlation, or conducting an outright analysis of any underlying causal relationship between either a single attribute and the value (usually measured by price) or multiple characteristics and the value.

This project involved developing a web application which uses client data to create visualizations for business decision-making.
